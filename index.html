COLLECTION

As huge comic book fans, we were inspired to create a collection that represents that exact art style. We developed 10 different savages, which would represent the different profiles we believe are a good fit for our tribe. We are extremely grateful for the dedication of our artist – Konstantin Vitkov for creating such an amazing collection and concept art for our meta world. Each character will have 1000 unique variations and that would make up our collection of 10000 NFT’s. Our goal for the artwork was to create a blend between a post-apocalyptic Mad Max-style, with a more digital Cyberpunk twist, representing our tribe of digital Web 3.0 rebels. Our characters will be developed into fully functional 3D models, which you can use as avatars in Metaverse, where you’ll have the option to pimp them out even further. Holding one of our NFT’s will give you access to our Metaverse, as well as other perks which will be revealed soon.

THE ARTIST

Konstantin Vitkov-Titis

Comic book artist

Our NFT's have been hand drawn by one of the top comic book artists in Europe – Konstantin Vitkov.

Truly a savage in his craft and a person with who we are more than proud to work. A loving husband and a father, a die-hard Mike Mignola fan, and a full-time SAVAGE!

He started his journey into the art world at the age of 4 and has not stopped creating amazing worlds for us ever since, even publishing his own comic book Jack Eridon and his latest hit, Kamikaze.

To add to this impressive list, our fellow savage has already built an impressive track record on his IMDB page working for movies such as Rambo(2008), the Expandables, Olympus has Fallen, and many more. What drove him to work in the NFT fields is the possibility for him to present himself and his work within this amazing new platform, as well as the creative freedom he gets working on this project.

Building our metaverse was our highest priority, we wanted to have it finished before we launch the project – mission accomplished, it is ready!
